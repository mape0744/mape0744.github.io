Challenge 5: Accessibility Statement

These challenges may have caused a few accessibility issues, although there are not many. In terms of the forms in challenge 3, someone who is color blind will likely not be able to pick their two favorite colors. To solve this issue I would either take the question out as a a whole or create another question that could replace this. I think the best way to approach this accesibility issue would be to add an audio feature that allows those who are visually impaired to also answer or understand the form.
